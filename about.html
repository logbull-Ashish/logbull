About Us

LogBull IT Solutions Private Limited is Cloud IT services and solutions company. LBIT is a Customer focussed, Cloud Computing and Cloud Based Solution and Services provider focussed on Architecting and Executing reliable, secure and optimized business ready Cloud solutions for our High-Tech customers in various domains like Media, Education, E-Commerce. We offer end-to-end, turnkey solutions for enterprise, small-to-medium sized businesses (SMBs) to our world wide and multi-national customers. We also partner with our Customers to optimize their Application based on Java, PHP, MySQL on PHP frameworks, ensuring high performance and memory optimization in Cloud and get the optimal leverage of their investment in Cloud.

We have progressed to be AWS Advance Partner this year based on our previous years track record of success in Architecting and production deployment of medium to larger Customer Cloud setups. Basically enabling Customers efficiently to have Amazon Cloud as their backbone and in helping them to effectively leverage the benefits of a robust, reliable, secure and on demand Cloud infrastructure.

Our vision at LBIT is to provide innovative Cloud IT services and solutions to customers with integrity to create a longer term relationship, which creates customers reliance on us. Our mission at LBIT is to serve customers with innovative, efficient and profitable solutions in a cost effective manner so as to provide highest value to our customers. Our Leadership team has vast experience In serving worldwide customers on-site and offshore across most global geographies. The technical team has more than 25 years of experience serving world wide and multi-national customers in the areas of Cloud Computing, IT infrastructure, Java Application development and optimization etc.
