  • Experience with the Amazon AWS or Microsoft Azure clouds will be an added advantage.
  • Experience with Microsoft infrastructure including Exchange, Office 365, Active Directory, DHCP, SSO, Sharepoint etc will be an added advantage.
  • Work with cross-team collaboration and communication across technical disciplines.

  • Degree or Masters in IT, Computer Science or Telecommunications.
